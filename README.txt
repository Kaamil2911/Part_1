Project Name: Creme de la Creme

Organization Overview:
History
Creme de la creme is a French inspired bakery that truly encapsulates the essence of what a high quality baked good should be. It was established in Monaco, a small country to the South of France. A family run business made initially to bring new ideas and new flavours to Fontvielle (city) as there was a lack of unique products at the time.
Mission
Our mission is to preserve the quality and tradition of french baking by providing the community with premium baked products.
Vision
Our vision is to expand our products and expertise to other parts of the world.

Target Audience
We intend to target individuals that have a taste for premium baked goods, specifically young adults all the way to older members (20 +).


Website Goals and Objectives

Goals
The goal for this website is to generate potential customers through seeing the website, allow for the purchase and delivery of products  and provide information about specials, new products and other valuable information.

KPI’s
Website traffic - To understand how many people visit certain pages of the website
Click-through rates - How many times a certain product or link was clicked in order to understand the value of said link/product.
Social media traffic - How often are customers or blog posts engaging with this website online.

Website Features and Functionality
This website will have a homepage, about us, contact, product page and a FAQ. This website will have a search bar to look for products and a sign in portal.
